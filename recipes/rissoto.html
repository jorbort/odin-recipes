Risotto

creamy dreamy risotto

a very creamy risotto

Ingredients

  • arborio rise
  • checken broth
  • shalots
  • mushrooms
  • white wine
  • parmesan cheese
  • butter

Step by Step

  • on pan put the shallot to cook with a bit of olive oil and salt
  • when shallots start to brown and toast add the mushhroom
  • cook for a few minutes then add the withe wine and let it cook the onions with the mushrooms util alcohol evaporates
  • add the rise and cook for a few minutes, until edges become translucent
  • add the broth and let cook until it is almost absorbed by the rise
  • add the butter and the parmesan to taste
