Pesto tagliatelle

sicilian pesto tagliatelle

homemade pesto

Ingredients

  • basil
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • parmesan
  • fresh tagliatelle
  • lemon

Step by step

  • cook the basil for 2 minutes in boiling water,inmediately after put it in iced water
  • in a blender or food processor put the basil garlic and the juice of one lemon
  • start processing it, and little by little pour the olive oil
  • once it is all ready put the parmesan little by little
  • cook the pasta for 3 minutes in boiling water
  • integrate the pasta with pesto, put a few basil leaves on top and some parmesan for decoration
