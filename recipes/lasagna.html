Dlicious lasagna

A delicious home made traditional italian beef lasagna

Ingredients

  • tomatoe sauce
  • ground beef
  • mozzarella
  • pasta dough
  • beixamel sauce

Step by step

  • Pre-heat the oven at 210 celsius degree
  • in a hot pan cook the meat
  • when the meat is fully cooked add the tomatoe suace leaving about 1/4 apart for later
  • in a caserole put some sauce on the bottom and cover with one layer of pasta, on top of it put the meat with the sauce and cover it with a little bit of beixamel,put an other layer of pasta to cover it
  • repeat the process above till you have all the layers ready,put the mozzarella on top
  • put in the oven for 12 minutes and check if the chesse is properly melted if needed add more time
